# Deliverable 1: Business Task

## Business Task
Determine how annual members and casual riders use Cyclistic bikes differently, and use those insights to inform a marketing strategy that converts casual riders into annual members.

## Key Stakeholders
- **Lily Moreno (Director of Marketing):** Owns marketing strategy and campaign direction.
- **Cyclistic Marketing Analytics Team:** Executes data analysis and reporting.
- **Cyclistic Executive Team:** Approves the final recommendations.

## How Insights Drive Decisions
Understanding when and how each user type rides (commute vs leisure, weekday vs weekend, trip duration, and station patterns) enables targeted campaigns and product positioning that maximize member conversion and lifetime value.
